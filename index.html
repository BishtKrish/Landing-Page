ABOUT THIS GAME

There was once a time when goblins were plentiful in the world. That is, until the adventurers came, bringing with them an unquenchable thirst for experience points and loot. Now on the brink of extinction, it’s time for the goblins to fight back! Gather a ragtag group of goblins and journey through an epic fantasy world, as you guide them across a fully voice narrated adventure. Join their fight for survival in Goblin Stone, an epic turn-based RPG with a roguelike twist that is unique to your playthrough.
